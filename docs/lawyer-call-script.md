## Part 1: The Core Business (20 min)

### 1A. The Upload + Process Model

"Users upload audio files, my AI separates them into individual instruments, and they practice along. Companies like Moises and Chordify do the same thing. What do I need to have in place to operate the way they do?"

*What you're looking for: Confirmation the model is legal. She should mention DMCA safe harbor, user responsibility, ToS language.*

### 1B. Stem Caching

"After processing, I store the separated stems on my server so users can come back to them. What's the right approach — per-user storage, auto-deletion after a certain period, or something else?"

*Don't mention cross-user caching unless she asks. Let her define the boundaries.*

### 1C. Upload Consent

"I've built a consent popup that users see before their first upload — 'you confirm you own this content or have the right to use it.' Is that enough, or does it need specific legal language?"

*You already built this. Get her to approve the wording or give you better language.*

*DON'T say you scraped them. DON'T mention UG. Just describe what you have and let her advise.*

### 2B. Third-Party Tab Data

*DON'T mention Songsterr's ToS specifically. Let her identify the issue.*

### 2C. Archive.org Live Music

"We have an integration with Archive.org's live music archive — bands like Grateful Dead that allow taping. Users can browse and process those recordings. Any concerns there?"

*This should be clean but get her confirmation.*

## Part 3: DMCA & Compliance (15 min)

### 3A. DMCA Setup Review

"I've registered a DMCA agent, have a ToS, Privacy Policy, and a takedown process. What am I missing? Do I need a repeat infringer policy?"

*Get a specific checklist of gaps.*

### 3B. ToS Review

"Would you be willing to review my Terms of Service and flag anything that needs changing? What would that cost?"

*She may include this in the $800 or quote separately. Either way, get a number.*

### 3C. Safe Harbor

"Walk me through what I need for DMCA safe harbor — am I covered, and what would blow it?"

*This is the most important legal protection you have. Understand it fully.*
