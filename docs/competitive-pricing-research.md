## Detailed Competitor Profiles

### Moises (moises.ai)
- **Market position:** Largest player, 70M+ users, $50M raised (2025)
- **Free:** 5 tracks/month, basic stem separation, ads
- **Premium ($3.99/mo):** Unlimited tracks, all instrument stems in premium quality, no ads
- **Pro ($9.99/mo):** HiFi quality stems, dialogue/music/SFX separation, batch processing, API access
- **Annual discounts:** ~30% off (Premium ~$2.74/mo, Pro ~$6.99/mo)
- **Teacher discount:** Standing discount available
- **Note:** Does NOT offer tab/chord transcription — separation and practice tools only

### LALAL.AI
- **Free:** 10 minutes in relaxed queue, 200MB upload limit, no downloads
- **Lite ($9.99/mo or $90/yr):** Unlimited relaxed queue, 90 fast-queue min/mo, 2GB uploads, batch processing, downloads
- **Pro ($19.99/mo or $180/yr):** 250 fast-queue min/mo, VST plugin, early access to features
- **Business top-ups (one-time):** Master $50 (750 min), Premium $190 (3,000 min), Enterprise $300 (5,000 min)
- **Note:** Pure stem separation — no transcription, no practice tools

### Klangio (klang.io)
- **Free:** 20-second demo transcriptions, unlimited tries
- **Pro (single app ~$14.99/mo, or ~$5.99/mo annually):** Full-song transcription, edit mode, PDF/MIDI/MusicXML/GuitarPro export
- **All apps bundle:** ~$49.99/yr (promotional)
- **14-day money-back guarantee**
- **Note:** Pure transcription — no stem separation. Supports Guitar2Tabs, Piano2Notes, Sing2Notes, Drum2Notes, Violin2Notes, Wind2Notes

### Chordify
- **Free:** Unlimited songs, basic chord detection from YouTube/Spotify/uploaded audio
- **Premium ($6.99/mo or ~$42/yr):** Transposition, MIDI export, collections, print chords, capo indicator
- **Premium+Toolkit ($4.50/mo, or $2.25/mo on annual):** All Premium + guitar tuner, metronome, fingering diagrams
- **Note:** Chord detection only — no stem separation, no full tab transcription

### Songsterr
- **Free:** Huge tab library, playback with 10-bar pauses, ads
- **Plus ($9.99/mo):** Uninterrupted playback, speed control, loop measures, AI transcription (50 tabs/mo), MIDI and Guitar Pro export, print tabs, solo/mute tracks, pitch shift, ad-free
- **Note:** Tab library + AI transcription, no stem separation

### Ultimate Guitar
- **Free:** Massive tab library, basic chords/tabs, ads
- **Pro ($9.99/mo or $29.99-$39.99/yr):** Guitar Pro tabs, official tabs, interactive playback, backing tracks, print, courses
- **Note:** World's largest tab library. No stem separation, no AI transcription from audio

### iZotope RX 11
- **Standard:** $389 one-time — 18 plugins, Music Rebalance (stem control), audio repair
- **Advanced:** $1,366 one-time — 20 plugins, all Standard features + advanced processing
- **Note:** Professional market. Not consumer-friendly for musicians wanting practice tools. Music Rebalance allows adjusting stem levels but isn't full separation export

### Splitter.ai
- **Free:** ~5 separations/month, slow processing mode
- **Pro (~$14.95/mo):** Near-perfect 2-stem separation, reverb removal, YouTube direct splitting, turbo mode, 1-day trial available
- **Note:** Stem separation only — no transcription or practice tools

### Melody.ml (Demucs online)
- **Free:** 2 songs included
- **Pay-per-song:** $0.50/song, bulk: 10/$5, 20/$10, 50/$20
- **Limits:** 100MB file size, 10-min max length, files available for 1 month
- **Note:** Cheapest option for occasional use. Demucs-based. No transcription

### Music Demixer (freemusicdemixer.com)
- **Free trial:** 3 days full access
- **Pro ($9.99/mo or $99.99/yr):** 6-stem separation (vocals, drums, bass, piano, guitar, other), transcription to sheet music (PDF, MusicXML, MIDI), 96% piano transcription accuracy
- **Note:** CLOSEST COMPETITOR TO STEMSCRIBE — combines stem separation AND transcription in one tool

### Yousician
- **Free:** Limited daily lessons, basic exercises
- **1 instrument ($19.99/mo or $119.99/yr):** Unlimited lessons for one instrument, real-time feedback, full song library
- **Premium+ all instruments ($29.99/mo or $179.99/yr):** All instruments (guitar, bass, ukulele, piano, singing), all features
- **Family plan available** for up to 4 accounts
- **Note:** Lesson/practice platform, not stem separation or transcription. Different market segment

## Key Market Insights

### Pricing Models in Music Tech
1. **Subscription (dominant):** Monthly $3.99-$29.99, annual discounts of 30-50%
2. **Per-song/per-minute:** Melody.ml ($0.50/song), LALAL.AI business ($0.04-0.10/min)
3. **One-time purchase:** iZotope RX ($389-$1,366) — pro market only
4. **Freemium with hard limits:** Most common approach — free tier with track/time limits

### What Musicians Will Pay
- Online music education market growing rapidly; app ecosystems captured 50.75% of revenue in 2025
- Users willing to pay ~$14.40/mo for platforms with strong personalization and social features
- Habit formation is key driver — daily practice tools command higher prices than occasional-use tools
- Price sensitivity is high in hobbyist segment ($5-10/mo sweet spot)
- Serious/semi-pro musicians tolerate $15-30/mo for comprehensive tools

### Who Combines Stem Separation + Transcription?
Very few competitors offer both in one tool:
1. **Music Demixer** — 6-stem separation + PDF/MIDI/MusicXML transcription ($9.99/mo)
2. **StemScriber** (us) — stem separation + chord detection + Guitar Pro export
3. **Moises** — stem separation + practice tools (but NO tab/chord transcription from audio)
4. **AudioShake** — stem separation + lyric transcription (B2B/enterprise, not consumer)

**This is StemScriber's key differentiator.** The combination of high-quality stem separation, chord/tab transcription, AND Guitar Pro export is essentially unique in the consumer market.

### Competitive Analysis

**Free tier (3 songs/mo):**
- GOOD — Moises gives 5, but most competitors give very little for free
- The 3-song limit is reasonable and competitive
- Consider: Could bump to 5 to match Moises without significant cost

**Pro tier ($10/mo, 25 songs):**
- WELL POSITIONED — Right in the sweet spot
- Moises Pro (comparable features) is $9.99/mo
- Songsterr Plus is $9.99/mo
- Music Demixer Pro is $9.99/mo
- $10 is the market consensus for a "serious hobbyist" tier
- 25 songs/mo is generous — most users won't hit this

**Premium tier ($29/mo, unlimited):**
- POSSIBLY TOO HIGH for consumer market
- Only Yousician Premium+ ($29.99) charges this much, and they're a full lesson platform
- LALAL.AI Pro is $19.99/mo with unlimited relaxed processing
- Most competitors top out at $15-20/mo
- Risk: conversion from Pro to Premium may be very low at this price delta ($10 -> $29)

### Recommendations

1. **Keep Free at 3 songs/mo** — Competitive. Drives conversion without being too generous.

2. **Keep Pro at $10/mo** — Market consensus price. Consider $8/mo annual ($96/yr) to drive annual commitments.

3. **Reduce Premium to $19-20/mo** — Better aligned with market. The jump from $10 to $29 is too steep.
   - Alternative: Add a middle tier at $19/mo (50 songs) and keep $29 for truly unlimited + API access
   - Annual pricing: $16/mo ($192/yr) or $14/mo ($168/yr)

4. **Add annual pricing with 30-40% discount** — Every competitor does this. Critical for reducing churn.
   - Free: stays free
   - Pro: $10/mo or $72/yr ($6/mo) — 40% discount
   - Premium: $20/mo or $144/yr ($12/mo) — 40% discount

5. **Consider per-song top-ups** for free users — Melody.ml's $0.50/song model could work as an upsell for users who don't want subscriptions. Even $1-2/song could convert users who need one-off processing.

6. **Emphasize the unique value proposition in marketing:** "The only tool that separates stems AND transcribes tabs in one workflow" — this is a genuine gap in the market that justifies premium pricing.
