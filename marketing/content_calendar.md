### Day 11 — Thursday, March 19
**Platform:** Facebook
**Pillar:** Musician Tips — Practice Method
**Post (new):**

> Here's a practice hack we've been using with StemScriber:
>
> 1. Upload the song you're trying to learn
> 2. Isolate just your instrument's stem
> 3. Slow it down to 50% (no pitch change)
> 4. Loop the section that's giving you trouble
> 5. Read along with the auto-generated tab
>
> It's basically what a private lesson looks like, minus the $60/hour.
>
> The AI-generated tab isn't always 100% note-for-note, but combined with actually hearing the isolated part slowed down? You'll nail it way faster than guessing from the full mix.
>
> Beta invites: DM us.

**Format:** Text post
**Notes:** Practical value post. Positions StemScriber as a practice tool, not just a novelty.
