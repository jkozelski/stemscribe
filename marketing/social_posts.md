### FB Post 4 — The Technical Flex (Casual)

What StemScriber actually does under the hood:

1. You upload a song (or paste a YouTube link)
2. AI separates it into 6 individual stems — vocals, guitar, bass, drums, piano, other
3. Each stem gets transcribed to MIDI using neural networks trained on real instrument recordings
4. The MIDI gets converted into a Guitar Pro (.gp5) tab file
5. You also get chord detection and key analysis for the full track

No other tool does all of that in one pipeline. Most make you bounce between 2-3 different apps and $37/month in subscriptions.

We're in beta. DM for an invite code — musicians get priority.
