1. Overview

StemScriber (stemscriber.com) is an audio stem separation and music practice web application operated by Kozelski Enterprises LLC, based in Charleston, South Carolina, United States. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

By accessing or using StemScriber, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the service.

3. How Audio Is Processed and Stored

Your audio is processed on our own infrastructure. We do not send your audio files to third-party AI services, and we do not use your uploads to train machine learning models.

When you upload a song:

  1. Upload: Your audio file is received by our server.
  2. Stem separation: AI models running on our GPU infrastructure separate the audio into individual stems (vocals, drums, bass, guitar, piano).
  3. Transcription: Separated stems are analyzed to generate chord charts, notation, and MIDI data.
  4. Caching: Your original audio and separated stems are cached on our servers so you can return to practice without re-processing.
  5. Access: Results are available in your library whenever you log in.

Key privacy safeguards:

  • Audio files are processed on our own servers — not sent to any third-party AI service.
  • We do not use your uploaded audio to train, improve, or fine-tune any machine learning models.
  • We do not share your audio files or outputs with any third party.
  • Your library is private — other users cannot see or access your songs.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for these purposes only:

  • Account management: Authenticate your identity and manage your subscription.

  • Communication: Send you important account-related messages (e.g., password resets, service updates).
  • Security: Detect and prevent abuse, rate-limit requests, and protect service integrity.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with data brokers. Your audio, your account information, and your usage data stay with us and are used solely to provide the Service to you.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Audio Files & Stems

  • Uploaded audio files are automatically deleted within 48 hours of job completion.
  • Generated outputs (separated stems, MIDI, tablature, chord charts) are automatically deleted within 7 days of job completion.
  • You can request earlier deletion of any or all of your cached files at any time by emailing support@stemscriber.com.
  • If your account is deleted, all associated audio and stems are deleted from our servers.

Account Data

  • Your account information (email, preferences) is retained as long as your account is active.
  • After account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

Server Logs

  • Server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, request paths) are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging, then purged.

Requesting Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@stemscriber.com. We will fulfill your request within 30 days.

8. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication: Account access is secured via JWT tokens through Supabase.
  • Access control: Server access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Rate limiting: API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse.
  • Library privacy: Your song library is private — other users cannot access your songs or stems.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR (EEA/UK Users)

  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is consent-based, withdraw it at any time.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: Processing your audio is necessary to provide the service you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: Server logging for security and reliability.

To exercise your rights, email support@stemscriber.com. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Cookies & Local Storage
