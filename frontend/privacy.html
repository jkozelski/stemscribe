3. How Audio Is Processed

Your audio never leaves our processing infrastructure. We run our own audio processing models on our own hardware.

When you upload a song or provide a YouTube URL:

  1. Upload/download: The audio file is received by our server or downloaded temporarily from the provided URL.
  2. Stem separation: Audio processing models (RoFormer or Demucs) running on our local hardware separate the audio into individual instrument stems (vocals, drums, bass, guitar, piano, other).
  3. Transcription: Separated stems are transcribed into MIDI notation using our own transcription models.
  4. Output generation: MIDI data is converted into Guitar Pro (.gp5) tablature files.
  5. Delivery: Results (stems, MIDI, tablature) are made available for you to download or save to Google Drive.

Key privacy safeguards for audio processing:

  • Audio files are processed on our own servers — they are not sent to any third-party service (not OpenAI, not Google, not Amazon).
  • We do not use your uploaded audio to train, improve, or fine-tune any machine learning models.
  • We do not share your audio files or generated outputs with any third party.
  • Temporary audio files from YouTube URL processing are deleted after processing completes.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Provide the service: Process your audio files and deliver stem separation, transcription, and tablature results.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Audio Files & Processing Results

  • Uploaded audio files are retained on our server only for the duration necessary to complete processing. Once results are delivered, source audio may be cached temporarily for your convenience and is automatically purged.
  • Generated stems, MIDI, and tablature files are available for download for a limited period after processing. They are automatically deleted from our servers thereafter.
  • YouTube-sourced audio is deleted immediately after processing completes.

Beta Invite Data

  • Beta codes, associated names, and redemption dates are stored for the duration of the beta program and will be deleted when the beta concludes or when you request deletion.

Server Logs

  • Standard server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, request paths) are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes, then purged.

Requesting Deletion

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time by emailing stemscriber@gmail.com. We will fulfill your request within 30 days.

7. Security Measures

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All connections to StemScriber are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
