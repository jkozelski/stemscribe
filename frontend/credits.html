Open Source Credits

StemScriber stands on the shoulders of the open source community. Every stem, every note, every lyric line is powered by world-class research released freely to the world. We're grateful to these projects and the teams behind them.

Demucs — Meta AI Research
State-of-the-art music source separation model. Splits any song into up to 6 clean stems in seconds using deep learning trained on thousands of hours of music.
Whisper — OpenAI
General-purpose speech recognition model trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual data. Powers our vocal transcription and lyric alignment pipeline.
Basic Pitch — Spotify Research
Audio-to-MIDI transcription using a lightweight neural network. Converts isolated instrument stems into accurate MIDI and Guitar Pro tablature.
AlphaTab — Open Source
Cross-platform music notation and tablature rendering engine. Powers the Practice Mode viewer — renders Guitar Pro files directly in the browser with zero plugins.
Songsterr — Guitar Tab Reference
Professional guitar and bass tablature used as a reference alongside our AI transcription. When available, provides human-verified tabs you can compare against.

StemScriber is built with ❤️ on top of open source. If you're a researcher or developer behind any of these projects — thank you.
