How It Works

Three Steps. Any Song.

From a recording to playable tabs in minutes. No manual transcription, no guesswork.

1
Upload or Paste a Link
Drop an audio file or paste a YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, or SoundCloud link. We handle the rest.
2
Separates & Transcribes
StemScriber isolates 6 stems (vocals, guitar, bass, drums, piano, other) and transcribes each instrument to MIDI and Guitar Pro tabs.
3
Play, Practice, Download
Mix stems, slow things down, loop tricky sections, detect chords, and export Guitar Pro files. Learn from the masters.
