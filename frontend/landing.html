How It Works

Three Steps. Any Song.

From a recording to a playable chord chart in minutes. No manual transcription, no guesswork.

1
Drop Your Track
Upload any audio file — MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A. Got a song? We'll split it into every instrument.
2
Separates & Charts
StemScriber isolates 6 stems (vocals, guitar, bass, drums, piano, other) and generates a professional chord chart with diagrams, lyrics, and timing.
3
Play, Practice, Download
Mix stems, slow things down, loop tricky sections, and learn from a chord chart that auto-scrolls with the song.
