- Analyze usage patterns to improve the product (aggregated, anonymized)

## 4. Audio File Handling

4.1. **Uploaded audio files** are stored temporarily on our servers solely for the purpose of processing (stem separation, transcription, etc.).

4.2. **Processed output files** (stems, MIDI, notation) are stored for download for up to [30 days] after processing, then automatically deleted.

4.3. **We do NOT:**
   - Listen to, review, or manually access your uploaded audio
   - Use your audio to train AI models (unless you explicitly opt in)
   - Share your audio with third parties
   - Create a permanent archive of your uploads

4.4. You may request immediate deletion of your files at any time by contacting support or through your account settings.
